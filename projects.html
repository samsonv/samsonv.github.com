Making a simple static HTML5 webpage layout with Boilerplate and LESS.

I like designing, but I'm not a savvy HTML or CSS-coder. And I don't have any great plans of becomming one - I really hate trying to figure out why a web page doesn't renders as I want. Boilerplate gives you a great startingpoint: A simple and standard web page-layout, with a comprehensive stylesheet with most of quirky browser differencies ironed out. You can also customized the template before downloading with - best of all - the ability to choose LESS stylesheets. LESS is pure genius for people who don't want to learn css and want to make up a usable design quick. I found this guide really helpful.
